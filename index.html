FAQ

Questions answered

Everything you want to know before applying.

How soon will I see results? ▾
Most artists see measurable growth within 3–4 weeks of their campaign launching. Playlist pitching results can come within 7 days of a release. Ad campaigns start generating data from Day 1 — we share weekly updates so you're never in the dark.
Is ad spend included in the price? ▾
Starter: no ad spend included — it's a service-only plan focused on organic growth channels like playlisting and content strategy. Growth: includes ₹10,000 ad spend (Meta/IG). Pro: includes ₹40,000 ad spend (₹25K Meta + ₹15K Spotify). All ad spend goes directly into your campaigns — none of it is our fee.
What if I'm not happy with the results? ▾
We do a 30-day check-in on every campaign. If metrics aren't on track, we adjust strategy at no extra charge. Music marketing takes time, but we're accountable to results — if your campaign isn't delivering despite full effort from both sides, we'll have an honest conversation about what changes are needed.
Do I need to already be on Grootin? ▾
Yes — Loudr is built exclusively for artists on the Grootin distribution platform. If you're not on Grootin yet, signing up is free and takes just a few minutes at grootin.in. Once you have at least one release live, you're eligible to apply for Loudr.
Can I cancel my plan? ▾
Plans run on a 3-month minimum commitment — this is because real music marketing takes time to build momentum and deliver meaningful results. After the initial 3 months, plans are month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. We're not here to lock you in; we want you to stay because the results are worth it.
What genres do you work with? ▾
All genres. We have experience with Hip-hop, Pop, R&B, EDM, Indie, Punjabi, Bollywood, Folk, Classical Fusion, Rock and more. Marketing strategy is tailored specifically to your genre and target audience — there is no one-size-fits-all approach here.
